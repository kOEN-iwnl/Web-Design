A Brief History of the Internet

So Who Invented the Internet?

When it comes to the person responsible for the creation of the internet, there are many scientists who came up with the idea. MIT's J.C.R. Licklider popularized the idea of a network of computers. But it was ultimately Robert Taylor who use the idea and created ARPANET.

Early origins

While Licklider did popularize the idea, there were people before him who came up with the idea. Nikola Tesla had imagined a "world wireless system," but other visionary thinkers like Paul Otlet and Vannevar Bush imagined searchable storage systems of books for the internet.
