High School

Along with providing an elementary to middle school education, Morrison Academy also provides a high school education to its students. We have a great reputation for sending our students to great universities abroad, along with educating our students about Jesus Christ.

Courses

Morrison Academy has an extensive educational program, which covers the basics and more for each grade. We strive to go above and beyond, providing our students with Bible classes as well as normal everyday classes. We also provide AP (Advanced Placement) courses for our high school students.

Science Building

We also have the Science Buildings, an extensive facility for science-related courses, ranging from Biology to Chemistry. Here, we experiment safely in our multiple labs.
