MORRISON ACADEMY TAICHUNG

Introduction

Morrison Academy is a K-12 Christian International school. We provide a great and wholesome learning environment. Furthermore, we make it our mission to bring children to Christ in the hopes that they too will spread the Word.

Morrison Academy has campuses in Taipei, Taichung, Kaohsiung, and now, Chiayi. Established in 1952, Morrison has educated many students about the love of Christ.

Morrison Academy strives to pursue academic excellence and athletic achievements. Our courses provide a wholesome education for students that provide them for the future.
