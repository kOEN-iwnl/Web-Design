Sports Facilities

Morrison Academy has many sports facilities that students can access easily. Morrison Academy has multiple ball courts, fields. As well as a track, aquatic center, and weight room. Read on for further Detail.

Basketball Courts

Basketball Courts: Morrison Academy has three basketball courts in total. 2 of which are in our gymnasium. The other one is in the playground, otherwise known as the covered play area. The covered play area is mostly used as a play ground, whereas the other two gyms are more for middle and high school athletics.

Soccer Fields

Soccer Fields: Morrison Academy has 3 soccer fields. The field nearest to the playground is used as a play area for recess. The further field, and the field surrounded by the trackare used more for tournaments and middle and high school athletics.

Track

Track: Morrison Academy has a track surrounding a soccer field. The track is 400 meters long, and has 7 lanes. The track is open to all Morrison staff and students.

Aquatic Center

Aquatic Center: Morrison Academy has a 25 meter outdoor swimming pool, consisting of 6 lanes. There is also a baby pool, for those who are too young to enter the main pool. The Morrison Aquatic center has hosted multiple tournaments.

Weight Room

Weight Room: Morrison Academy's weight room has many machines one can choose from. There are many weights and equipment to ensure a strong and safe environment. The weight room is open to Morrison residents and students.
